Really Contributor License Agreement - CLA
==========================================

Thank you for your interest in contributing to a really project!

**Step 1:**
If you are contributing as an individual then download, review, and sign the Individual CLA. If you are contributing through your employer then have them download, review, and sign the Corporate CLA.

.. toctree::

   corporate-cla
   individual-cla

**Step 2:**
Once you or your employer has agreed to the CLA then you will need to confirm with your GitHub account.
